🎓 NEW FEATURE: AUTOMATIC DEPARTMENT-BASED SCHEDULE ASSIGNMENT

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ WHAT CHANGED:

BEFORE:
  ❌ Lecturer manually selects each student (checkbox list)
  ❌ Time-consuming for large classes
  ❌ Easy to forget students
  ❌ Not scalable

NOW:
  ✅ Lecturer enters department name (e.g., "Computer Science")
  ✅ ALL students in that department automatically assigned
  ✅ Fast and efficient
  ✅ No one gets left out
  ✅ Scalable to any number of students

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🔄 HOW IT WORKS:

1. Lecturer creates schedule
2. Enters department (e.g., "Computer Science")
3. Optionally enters batch (e.g., "2024")
4. System finds ALL students matching:
   - Department = "Computer Science"
   - Batch = "2024" (if specified)
5. Automatically assigns schedule to them
6. Creates reminders for all assigned students
7. Students see schedule in their dashboard

👨‍🏫 FOR LECTURERS:

Creating a Schedule:
  1. Go to Dashboard → "Create New Schedule"
  2. Fill in:
     - Subject Name: e.g., "Data Structures"
     - Topic: e.g., "Binary Trees"
     - Department: e.g., "Computer Science" (REQUIRED)
     - Date & Time
     - Batch: Optional (e.g., "2024")
  3. Click "Save Schedule"
  4. Done! All students in that department automatically assigned

Benefits:
  ✅ Faster - No manual selection
  ✅ Easier - Just type department
  ✅ Automatic - System handles everything
  ✅ No mistakes - All students included

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
